About Us

Every Great Holiday Deserves an Unforgettable Adventure.

As Garazor Watersports, we offer our guests not only water sports but also memories they will cherish for years on the magnificent Mediterranean coast of Alanya.

Every guest is special to us. You might be riding a Jet Ski for the first time or parasailing over the sea. No matter your experience, our professional team welcomes you safely, informs you at every stage, and ensures you experience one of the most exciting moments of your holiday.

Safety is our priority, and guest satisfaction is our greatest motivation. Therefore, all our equipment is regularly checked, maintained in accordance with international safety standards, and all our activities are managed by our experienced team.

From Jet Ski to Parasailing, Banana Boat to Fly Fish and Ringo, every activity combines speed, fun, and the fascinating view of the Mediterranean. Whether you are traveling with your family, friends, or alone, there is an unforgettable experience for everyone at Garazor Watersports.

Over the years, we have hosted thousands of guests from all over the world. Our greatest pride is our guests who visit us again every season and recommend us to their friends.

Because we don't just offer water sports. We create the best memory of people's holidays.

We invite you to feel freedom in the turquoise waters of the Mediterranean, experience adrenaline-filled moments, and become a part of the Garazor Watersports family.
